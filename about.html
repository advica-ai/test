About ADVICA – Autonomous Drone for Virtual Intelligent Care Assistance

ADVICA (Autonomous Drone for Virtual Intelligent Care Assistance) is a next-generation emergency response system that reimagines how critical medical aid is delivered — particularly in remote, disaster-affected, or inaccessible regions.

Powered by AI and 5G connectivity, ADVICA is equipped with voice-assisted communication, wireless health sensors, a live video feed, and obstacle-avoidance technology. It autonomously navigates to the victim’s location, enabling doctors to assess the situation in real time and offer immediate virtual consultation.

By drastically reducing response time — a crucial factor in saving lives during emergencies such as heart attacks, strokes, accidents, and natural disasters — ADVICA ensures that medical help reaches faster than traditional methods. It also supports remote health monitoring, AI-guided self-care, and is future-ready with features like automated medicine delivery through a secondary drone.

Tailored to India’s diverse terrain and healthcare access challenges, ADVICA offers a scalable, intelligent, and life-saving solution that bridges the critical gap between incident and care.

Our mission is simple yet vital: to deliver fast, safe, and reliable medical assistance—anytime, anywhere. Backed by cutting-edge technology and a strong commitment to innovation, ADVICA is redefining emergency healthcare in India.

With ADVICA, rapid medical response is no longer a challenge — it's a reality.
