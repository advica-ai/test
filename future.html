Future plan:

As ADVICA evolves, we aim to incorporate the following advancements to enhance emergency response capabilities:

1. Dual Drone Deployment:
In critical emergencies, two drones will be dispatched sequentially:

-First Drone: Will reach the site for immediate health assessment using sensors and initiate live consultation via AI voice assistant and video feed.

-Second Drone: Will follow shortly with prescribed medicines or small medical equipment as directed by the doctor during the first assessment.


2. AI-Based Triage System:
AI will automatically assess the severity of the patientâ€™s condition based on vital signs and prioritize cases accordingly for faster intervention.


3. Auto Medicine Dispensing Module:
A built-in compartment in the second drone will carry standard emergency medicines with a secure and automated release system based on remote doctor instructions.


4. Integration with National Emergency Grids:
ADVICA will be connected with local hospitals, ambulances, and government health systems for synchronized action in disaster zones or mass casualty events.


5. Multi-Language Voice Assistant:
The AI assistant will support multiple Indian languages for better interaction with victims in diverse regions.


6. Real-time Navigation Updates:
Drones will adapt to live weather and terrain data to dynamically alter their route for fastest and safest arrival.


7. Night and Weather-Resistant Operations:
Improved sensors and body design to allow functioning in rain, fog, and low-light/night conditions.
